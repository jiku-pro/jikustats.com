Our Story

"Jiku" is Japanese for space & time, forming the essence of all modern, dynamic systems. The Jiku Core software, which powers all Jiku products, has been in development over the last 15 years, and has been recently validated in the Journal of Statistical Software.

​With thousands of downloads of the Jiku Core beta platform, we are now ready to introduce Jiku to the market to help advance analytical and innovation gaps that have existed in data-driven industries.

Our Vision

Jiku aims to offer an open-source statistical and computational core to better serve the statistics and analytics markets. Our cloud-based platform offers unique supercomputer speed and data storage possibilities, accessible from lightweight clients including smart devices. The future of statistics and analytics is the cloud, and we will help your organization improve data robustness, analysis efficiency, and automation.
